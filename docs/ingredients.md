# Recommendations of ingredients for cocktails that work well with Bartendro

## Verbotten Flüssigkeit
- carbonated drinks such as soda water, tonic water, coca-cola, soft drinks - bubbles do not work well with the pumps 
- Creamy drinks - Kahula, Baileys, Coconut cream - leaves sticky residue that rots the tubing
- non-filtered aromatic water / infusions with sediments - particulates can get trapped in tubes or pumps

## Strong booze
- Whiskey
- Bourbon
- Rum
- Gin
- Vodka
- Tequila
- Triple Sec
- Absinthe


## other booze

## Mixers
