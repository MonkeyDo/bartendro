`cat history`

circa 2000: Rob meets Pierre, they love good cocktails, prototypes were made

2009: The duo found Party Robotics LLC in California with the slogan "The future of cocktails is quality drinks poured repeatably, quickly and socially. Reduce your pour costs, and automate your mundane tasks."

2010: Pierre & Rob create the `Water (to Wine) Cooler` project together: [see video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gia1HzkdTlk)

2011: first post on Party Robotics repository in January: [see commit](https://github.com/partyrobotics/bartendro/commit/c256582fca02d3f02003087d9002b98abcbdd9c0)

2011: Prototyping of the Tequila Sunrise Bot done: [see video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ft8u4V5cH-U)

2013: Kickstarter campaign for Bartendro launched in March - [see video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bmmMHVFlmKw) - [see campaign](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/partyrobotics/bartendro-a-cocktail-dispensing-robot) where 486 backers pledged $197,464 to help bring this Bartendro to life. Kickstarted was fully funded in less than 14 days
 
2021: first pre-built SD card image released under Party Robotics: [see release](https://github.com/partyrobotics/bartendro/releases/tag/v-2021-05-21)
2022:
2023: Party Robotics LLC officially closes, Rob publishes [partyrobotics.github.com](partyrobotics.github.com) and redirects partyrobotics.com to it stating "Party Robotics is dead. Bartendro lives on! Party Robotics had a good run, but now what? Well, I, the co-founder of Party Robotics still have 2 bartendros at home and have regular parties. I’ll keep working to update and even improve the bartendro software, so that our bots may live on!"
2024: Rob hosts monthly Bartendro nights in his home in Barcelona, many a cocktails and friendships were made.
2025: idem. 
2026: Rob Kaye passes away, a Monkey and Cat inherited his Bartendro and sought to revive the code: this repo
